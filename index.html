Our Story

In 2022, OpenAI released ChatGPT, making powerful AI available for pennies for anyone to use.
Now we hurtle toward the future at speed. It's terrifying, but it also gives each of us the power to shape the world.
Or so I hope. I'm Christopher Smith, and I founded Promptly to build AI-powered software for small businesses and nonprofits.
